Silent Partner

CASE ID: VG-2024-0812

Case Overview: The Vanish Gallery Heist

At 02:14 AM, the "Eternal Sun" diamond was stolen from the Vanish Gallery's secure vault. Surveillance footage (partially obstructed) shows two suspects engaging in a violent struggle with the night security officer. One suspect was seen fleeing through the service exit, leaving behind traces of blood on the storage door. A single hair was recovered from the vault handle, and another was found on the officer's uniform. Your mission is to identify the "Silent Partner" who assisted Marcus Thorne in this high-stakes robbery.

Evidence Log

  • Location A (Vault): Impact spatter on North Wall; Hair Sample A (recovered from vault handle).
  • Location B (Service Exit): Impact spatter on Storage Door; Hair Sample B (recovered from officer's uniform).
  • DNA Sample A: Full profile recovered from hair follicle at Location A. Matches known associate Marcus Thorne.
  • DNA Sample B: Degraded profile recovered from blood at Location B. Suspected to belong to the second intruder.

Directives

  1. Determine Area of Origin for blood spatters at Locations A and B to confirm struggle positions.
  2. Compare Sample B's degraded DNA profile against the initial 7 suspects.
  3. Analyze medullary indices of hair samples to corroborate suspect physical data.
  4. Search the National Forensic Database (NFD) using Sample B's known alleles to identify the "Silent Partner".
