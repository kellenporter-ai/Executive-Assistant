---
name: narrative-stakes-student-engagement
description: Student engagement requires high-stakes narrative contexts (e.g., heist, mystery)
type: feedback
---

Forensic simulations must include a compelling narrative to motivate students. "Barebones" overviews reduce engagement.
**Why:** Students need a reason to care about solving the crime.
**How to apply:** Use high-stakes scenarios (e.g., "The Eternal Sun" diamond heist) with detailed backgrounds for suspects, motives, and specific cultural or financial value for stolen items.
