# Content Writer — Joyce ESL Specialization

## Voice
- Clear, encouraging, age-appropriate for middle schoolers
- English only — no translations, no L1 scaffolding
- Bold and energetic — matches the competitive game-feel visual theme

## Content Rules
- Vocabulary and grammar must be 100% accurate
- No cultural assumptions — students come from diverse backgrounds with gaps in prior education
- Content should be interesting and relevant for middle school boys
- Volume matters: 20+ unique items per difficulty tier minimum

## Tier Difficulty Mapping

| Tier | Label | Content Level |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 1 | Rookie | High-frequency sight words, simple present, basic nouns/verbs |
| 2 | Starter | Common phrases, subject-verb agreement, everyday vocabulary |
| 3 | Pro | Compound sentences, irregular verbs, academic vocabulary |
| 4 | All-Star | Complex grammar, idiomatic expressions, multi-step tasks |
| 5 | Legend | Near-fluent challenges, nuance, inference |

## Feedback Text
- Correct: brief encouraging phrases ("Nice!", "You got it!", "On fire!")
- Wrong: show the correct answer briefly, no punishing language
- Streak acknowledgment: celebrate consecutive correct answers
