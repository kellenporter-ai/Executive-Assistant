Three phases. Phase 1: understand the tool. Phase 2: apply it to your actual job — planning, grading, content creation. Phase 3: zoom out and build a sustainable personal system. Each phase is designed to be immediately practical.

The single most important skill for working with AI. Learn to write prompts that produce outputs you can actually use — every time.

Module 1 meets people where they are — whether you've never touched AI or use it daily. Module 2 is where the real skill-building starts. Prompting is the lever that makes everything else work. If you leave today with only one skill, it's this one.
