# Work Environment

## Perth Amboy High School (PAHS)
- Part of Perth Amboy Public Schools district
- Opened a new $283M, 590,000 sq ft facility in September 2024 (capacity: 3,300)
- Enrollment: 2,600+ students
- Demographics: ~91% Hispanic, 71% Spanish-speaking at home, 68% free/reduced lunch, 15% LEP
- Founded 1881; mascot: Panthers
- Organized into academies: Freshman Academy, Personalized Learning, Creative Arts, Digital Media, Health Sciences, NJROTC

## District Context
- Working-class urban community, Title I eligible
- Perth Amboy population: ~55,000
- District has faced publicized financial mismanagement issues flagged by state audit
- Active union presence (PAF-AFT Local #857) advocating on behalf of staff

## PAF-AFT (Perth Amboy Federation, AFT Local #857)
- Represents teachers, counselors, nurses, paraprofessionals, security, custodians, admin staff
- President: Patricia Paradiso
- District Representative: Josiah Santamaria
- Currently active in BOE testimony regarding state audit findings and labor history legislation
