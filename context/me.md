- **Time Zone:** Eastern Time (ET)
- **Teaching Philosophy:** Social Constructivism
- **Pedagogy:** ISLE (Investigative Science Learning Environment)
  - Students learn physics by mirroring authentic scientific practice
  - Observation > Pattern Recognition > Explanation > Predictive Testing > Evaluation & Revision
  - Multiple representations of physical processes
  - Growth mindset, perseverance, and physics identity development
  - Multiple attempts without punishment — reduce anxiety, build confidence
  - Core belief: everyone can learn physics given tools, time, and support
- **Courses Taught:** Honors Physics, AP Physics 1, Forensic Science
